Description

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken
  • mustard oil
  • butter
  • water
  • tomato
  • onion
  • green chilli
  • garam masala, red chilli masala
  • salt
  • garlic ginger paste
  • fresh cream
  • curd
  • lemon
  • salt
  • cashew
  • turmeric

    Steps

    1. mix chicken with curd, lemon, spices, giner-garlic paste, oil
    2. rest for 30 mins
    3. heat a pan with oil
    4. cook marinated chicken until lightly charred and fully cooked
    5. in same pan add butter and oil
    6. fry onions until soft and golden
    7. add garlic and garlic paste and cook for 1 min
    8. add tomato and cashews
    9. cook until tomatoes turn mushy and oil seperates
    10. blend into a paste
    11. add blended gravy back into pan
    12. add red chilli, coriander powder, garam masala and salt.
    13. add little water to adjust thickness
    14. add cooked chicken
    15. add cream and kasuri methi
    16. simmer for 5 mins on low flame
